# Best Sketch Comedy Shows

A curated list of the greatest sketch comedy shows of all time.

## Classic Era

### Monty Python's Flying Circus (1969-1974)
- **Country:** United Kingdom
- **Network:** BBC
- **Why it's great:** Revolutionary surreal humor that influenced generations of comedians. Featured the legendary Python troupe including John Cleese, Eric Idle, Terry Jones, Michael Palin, Graham Chapman, and Terry Gilliam.
- **Notable sketches:** Dead Parrot, Ministry of Silly Walks, The Spanish Inquisition, Argument Clinic

### Saturday Night Live (1975-Present)
- **Country:** United States
- **Network:** NBC
- **Why it's great:** The longest-running sketch comedy show in television history. Launched the careers of countless comedy legends and continues to be culturally relevant.
- **Notable cast members:** John Belushi, Dan Aykroyd, Eddie Murphy, Phil Hartman, Will Ferrell, Tina Fey, Amy Poehler, Kate McKinnon

### SCTV (1976-1984)
- **Country:** Canada
- **Network:** Various
- **Why it's great:** Brilliant parodies of television programming with memorable recurring characters. More character-driven than SNL.
- **Notable cast members:** John Candy, Eugene Levy, Catherine O'Hara, Rick Moranis, Martin Short, Andrea Martin

## Modern Classics

### Chappelle's Show (2003-2006)
- **Country:** United States
- **Network:** Comedy Central
- **Why it's great:** Fearless social commentary wrapped in hilarious sketches. Dave Chappelle's unique perspective on race, culture, and society.
- **Notable sketches:** Charlie Murphy's True Hollywood Stories, Rick James, Racial Draft, Clayton Bigsby

### Key & Peele (2012-2015)
- **Country:** United States
- **Network:** Comedy Central
- **Why it's great:** Cinematic production values, sharp social commentary, and incredible character work from Keegan-Michael Key and Jordan Peele.
- **Notable sketches:** Substitute Teacher, East/West Bowl, Obama's Anger Translator, Auction Block

### A Bit of Fry & Laurie (1989-1995)
- **Country:** United Kingdom
- **Network:** BBC
- **Why it's great:** Sophisticated wordplay and absurdist humor from Stephen Fry and Hugh Laurie. Showcased their incredible chemistry and versatility.
- **Notable sketches:** Vox Pops, Control & Tony, Soupy Twist

## Contemporary Excellence

### I Think You Should Leave with Tim Robinson (2019-Present)
- **Country:** United States
- **Network:** Netflix
- **Why it's great:** Aggressively weird and uncomfortable humor that commits fully to absurd premises. Cult favorite with incredibly quotable sketches.
- **Notable sketches:** Baby of the Year, Hot Dog Car, Coffin Flop, Tables

### A Black Lady Sketch Show (2019-Present)
- **Country:** United States
- **Network:** HBO
- **Why it's great:** Groundbreaking show featuring an all-Black female cast and writers room. Fresh perspectives and hilarious takes on everyday situations.
- **Creator:** Robin Thede

### Portlandia (2011-2018)
- **Country:** United States
- **Network:** IFC
- **Why it's great:** Gentle satire of hipster culture and Portland life. Fred Armisen and Carrie Brownstein's chemistry and character work.
- **Notable sketches:** Put a Bird on It, Women and Women First, Dream of the 90s

## International Gems

### The Kids in the Hall (1988-1995, 2022)
- **Country:** Canada
- **Network:** CBC/HBO
- **Why it's great:** Surreal, dark humor with all-male cast playing all roles. Influenced by Monty Python but with a unique Canadian sensibility.
- **Notable sketches:** Crushing Your Head, Chicken Lady, Buddy Cole

### Little Britain (2003-2006)
- **Country:** United Kingdom
- **Network:** BBC
- **Why it's great:** Character-based sketch show with memorable recurring characters. Matt Lucas and David Walliams created a gallery of British eccentrics.
- **Notable characters:** Vicky Pollard, Lou and Andy, Daffyd Thomas

### The State (1993-1995)
- **Country:** United States
- **Network:** MTV
- **Why it's great:** Influential sketch troupe that launched many successful comedy careers. Absurdist humor that was ahead of its time.
- **Notable members:** Michael Ian Black, Thomas Lennon, Robert Ben Garant, Ken Marino, Michael Showalter

## Honorable Mentions

- **The Carol Burnett Show** (1967-1978) - Variety show with brilliant sketch comedy
- **In Living Color** (1990-1994) - Groundbreaking diverse cast, launched the Wayans family
- **Mr. Show with Bob and David** (1995-1998) - Innovative format with interconnected sketches
- **The Whitest Kids U' Know** (2007-2011) - Dark, absurdist humor
- **Inside Amy Schumer** (2013-2016) - Sharp feminist comedy and social commentary
- **The Ben Stiller Show** (1992-1993) - Short-lived but influential
- **The Upright Citizens Brigade** (1998-2000) - Absurdist sketch comedy from UCB founders
- **That Mitchell and Webb Look** (2006-2010) - British sketch comedy with recurring characters

## What Makes Great Sketch Comedy?

1. **Strong Point of View** - The best sketch shows have a unique perspective
2. **Memorable Characters** - Recurring characters that audiences connect with
3. **Commitment to the Bit** - Fully committing to absurd premises
4. **Social Commentary** - Using humor to comment on society and culture
5. **Timing and Editing** - Knowing when to end a sketch
6. **Chemistry** - Cast members who work well together
7. **Originality** - Fresh takes and innovative formats
